Shiba Inu

Shiba Inu means "small dog" or "brushwood dog" in Japanese.

  • Life expectancy: 13 to 16 years
  • Height: 13.5 to 15.5 inches (female), 14.5 to 16.5 inches (male)
  • Weight: 17 pounds (female), 23 pounds (male)
  • Colors: black & tan, cream, red, red sesame

Shibas have existed since 300 b.c. They are the most popular breed in Japan. This breed is extremely intellegent and stubborn. While training, you can expect them to learn quickly, but they will often require treats as payment for good behavior. Shibas are very loyal and confident. They are great companion dogs. Socialize your Shiba and they will be the best dog you could ask for.
